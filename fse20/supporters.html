Supporters

FSE-20 offers the following Support Levels:

Diamond — $20,000 entitles the supporter to 10 registrants, the supporter's name on the FSE-20 memory stick, and an exhibition table.
Platinum — $10,000 entitles the supporter to 5 registrants and an exhibition table.
Gold — $5,000 entitles the supporter to 2 registrants and an exhibition table.
Silver — $2,500 entitles the supporter to 1 registrant plus recognition.
Bronze — $1,000 entitles the supporter to recognition.

If you're interested in becoming a supporter, please contact the General Chair, Will Tracz, will at tracz dot org.
