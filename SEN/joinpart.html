Joining, Subscriptions and Participation

To join ACM's Special Interest Group on Software Engineering (SIGSOFT), use one of these online forms:

  • SIGSOFT print and online membership
  • SIGSOFT online-only membership

You may also print the SIGSOFT membership application form (PDF) and return it by mail, or contact ACM, 2 Penn Plaza Suite 701, New York, NY 10121-0701, 1-212-869-7440.

The current annual rates in US dollars are:

  • SIGSOFT (Print and online) membership, Professional: $30
  • SIGSOFT (Print and online) membership, Student: $25
  • SIGSOFT (Print and online) membership, Affiliate (non-ACM Member): $30
  • SIGSOFT (online) membership, Professional: $20
  • SIGSOFT (online) membership, Student: $10
  • SIGSOFT (online) membership, Affiliate (non-ACM Member): $20
  • SIGSOFT Member Plus Package (in addition to SIGSOFT membership):
    • ICSE Proceedings: International Conference on Software Engineering (April): $30
    • Expedited Air (outside North America): $20

Print Memberships are mailed hardcopies of SEN. Online members receive access to pdf versions of SEN.

SIGSOFT dues include the cost of an annual subscription to Software Engineering Notes (SEN), either print and online, or online only. Back issues are available from the ACM Order Dept., P.O. Box 12114, Church Street Station, New York, NY 10257.

Members wishing to participate in SIGSOFT activities may contact the Chair or the Vice Chair. For other SIGSOFT-related business, contact James Peeples, ACM Headquarters, 1601 Broadway, 10th Floor, New York, NY 10019-7434, (1-212-626-0603), peeples@hq.acm.org.

Software Engineering Notes (ISSN 0163-5948) is published six times a year in January, March, May, July, September, and November by ACM.

For advertising information contact William R. Kooney, Account Executive, ACM, 2 Penn Plaza, Suite 701, New York, NY 10121-0701, (1-212-626-0687), kooney@hq.acm.org.
