Software Engineering Notes Online

The full text of current and all back issues of Software Engineering Notes (SEN) is available online as part of ACM's Digital Library. Access to SEN in the Digital Library is free for members of SIGSOFT; all you need is an ACM Web Account and password.

If you're a SIGSOFT member and you don't have a Web Account yet, set up your free Web Account first. While you're at it, why not create or update your ACM e-mail alias (yourname@acm.org) -- it's a free e-mail forwarding benefit for ACM members.
