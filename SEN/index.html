Software Engineering Notes (SEN) is edited, but not refereed. SEN is appropriate for research papers and reports in all areas of software engineering. SEN contributions are archived in the ACM Digital Library and are publicized through many search engines and scientific indices.

SEN submissions are now being accepted at http://sen.sigsoft.org/.


What's New

New Site for Online Submissions

We have a new site, http://sen.sigsoft.org/, for online submission of items for publication in SEN. You can submit articles, reports, reviews, columns, announcements and book reviews.
