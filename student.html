ACM SIGSOFT Information for Students

Benefits for student members of ACM SIGSOFT

  • Large discounts on registration at SIGSOFT events
  • A full year subscription to SIGSOFT's quarterly newsletter Software Engineering Notes (SEN) , which includes articles submitted by members and regular columns, including the popular forum "Risks to the Public"
  • Discounts on newsletters and proceedings
  • Networking and leadership opportunities through participation in SIGSOFT events, student and professional chapter meetings, and the ACM student website
  • Opportunities to serve as a volunteer in various capacities in support of SIGSOFT activities
  • Opportunities to be a student volunteer at an event in return for financial support to attend the event. Contact the general chair of the specific event for more information
  • Financial support to attend events through the Conference Attendance Program for Students (CAPS)
